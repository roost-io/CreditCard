Functional Test Cases:

- Credit Card Due Reminder:
    1. **Test Case 1** - Validate that the automated notification is sent to the cardholder before the payment due date.
        - Input: The system clock should reach the pre-determined timeframe before the payment due date.
        - Expected Output: Automated notification sent to cardholder.
    2. **Test Case 2** - Validate that the notification includes the last 4 digits of the credit card number.
        - Input: Automated notification.
        - Expected Output: Notification with last 4 digits of credit card number.
- Overdue Balance Alert:
    1. **Test Case 1** - Validate that an alert is sent to the cardholder if the payment due date is missed.
        - Input: The system clock should reach the day after the due date.
        - Expected Output: Alert sent to cardholder.
    2. **Test Case 2** - Validate that the overdue alert includes the last 4 digits of the credit card number.
        - Input: Overdue balance alert.
        - Expected Output: Alert with last 4 digits of credit card number.
- Collection Notification:
    1. **Test Case** - Validate that a notification is sent to the cardholder when the account becomes significantly delinquent.
        - Input: System detects the account is significantly delinquent.
        - Expected Output: Collection notification sent to cardholder.
- Payment Plan Proposal:
    1. **Test Case** - Validate that a proposal is sent to the cardholder when they are unable to pay the full overdue balance.
        - Input: System detects the cardholder is unable to pay the full overdue balance.
        - Expected Output: Payment plan proposal sent to cardholder.
- Collection Agency Involvement:
    1. **Test Case** - Validate that a collection agency is involved when the cardholder fails to respond to previous notifications and reminders.
        - Input: No response from the cardholder to all previous notifications.
        - Expected Output: Collection agency involvement notice sent to cardholder.
- Legal Action Initiation:
    1. **Test Case** - Validate that legal action is initiated in extreme cases of non-payment or default.
        - Input: System detects extreme case of non-payment or default.
        - Expected Output: Legal action initiation notice sent to cardholder.

Non-Functional Test Cases:

- **Security**:
    1. **Test Case** - Validate that only the last 4 digits of the credit card number are displayed in any communication or documentation.
        - Input: Any notification or document related to the collection process.
        - Expected Output: Only the last 4 digits of the credit card are visible.
    2. **Test Case** - Validate that the full credit card number is never shared or displayed in a plain text.
        - Input: Any notification or document related to the collection process.
        - Expected Output: Full credit card number is not visible/shared.
- **Performance**:
    1. **Test Case** - Validate the system's capability to send notifications to a large number of cardholders simultaneously.
        - Input: A large batch of notifications to be sent at the same moment.
        - Expected Output: All notifications are sent successfully without any delay/error.
- **Reliability**:
    1. **Test Case** - Validate the system's ability to function accurately and provide expected output under stipulated conditions consistently.
        - Input: Regular functioning of the system.
        - Expected Output: Consistent and expected output.
- **Usability**:
    1. **Test Case** - Validate that all messages sent to the cardholder are clearly understandable and the steps are easy to follow (if any).
        - Input: Any notification sent to cardholder.
        - Expected Output: Clear message, with easy to follow instructions if applicable.
